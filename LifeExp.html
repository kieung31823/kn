Life Expectancy Report


Project Link

Introduction

If born on the same day, are we not expected to die on the same day? We believe that our lifespan will not be same and some are naturally likely to live longer. In other words, life expectancy is different among us. The disparity in the duration of life may be influenced by many factors such as healthcare, social factors, or the country we live in.
- What are differences of Life Expectancy among countries and over time?
- What may factors impact on it?
This report will analyze life expectancy and its impact factors to answer those questions.

Description

This Interactive Life Expectancy Analytics Report allows you to drill into the data and explore all the factors that impact on Life Expectancy of 179 countries over the world during 2000-2015. It also analyzes the relationship between Life Expectancy and living condition factors which may impact on Life Expectancy in different perspectives and provides insights to efforts of improving global Life Expectancy.

Objectives

  • Differences in Life Expectancy
  • The greatest impacts on Life Expectancy
